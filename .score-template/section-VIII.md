Your

business

Lenders
 
and
 
investors
 
will

it

-
 
not
 
just
 
to

the

but
 
to

whether
 
your
 
thinking
 
is
 
sound
 
and
 
your
 
projections

in
 
reality.
 
A

plan
 
also

you
 
directly,
 
helping
 
you
 
set
 
clear
 
financial
 
goals,
 
anticipate
 
your
 
funding
 
needs,
 
and
 
make
 
smarter
 
decisions
 
as
 
your
 
business
 
grows.

Profit

The

projection
 
-

-

the

plan.
 
Download
 
the
 
12-Month
 
Profit
 
and
 
Loss
 
Projection
 
template
 
and
 
enter
 
your
 
projected
 
sales,
 
cost
 
of
 
goods

and
 
gross

(Draw
 
on

Sales
 
Forecast
 
you
 
built
 
in
 
Section
 
IV.)
 
Then
 
add
 
your

calculate
 
your
 
net

before

estimate

tax

and
 
arrive
 
at
 
your
 
net
 
operating
 
income.Don't
 
just
 
fill
 
in
 
the
 
numbers.
 
Explain
 
the
 
assumptions
 
behind
 
them.
 
How
 
did
 
you
 
arrive
 
at
 
your
 
revenue
 
projections?
 
How
 
did
 
you
 
estimate
 
costs?
 
Keep
 
detailed
 
notes
 
-
 
when
 
you
 
sit
 
down
 
with
 
a
 
lender
 
or
 
investor,
 
these
 
are
 
exactly
 
the
 
questions
 
they're
 
going
 
to
 
ask.

A

-

Some

investors
 
will
 
ask
 
for

Download

3-Year
 
Profit
 
and
 
Loss
 
Projection
 
template
 
if
 
this
 
applies
 
to
 
your
 
situation.

Cash
 
Flow
 
Projection
 
Profit
 
and
 
cash
 
flow
 
are
 
not
 
the

thing,
 
and

the
 
two
 
is

common
 
reason
 
otherwise

businesses

trouble.
 
Your

flow
 
projection
 
tracks
 
the
 
actual
 
movement
 
of
 
money
 
in
 
and
 
out
 
of
 
your
 
business
 
over
 
time:
 
when
 
you
 
need
 
to
 
pay
 
for
 
inventory,
 
rent,

-
 
and
 
when
 
you
 
can
 
realistically
 
expect
 
to
 
receive
 
payment
 
from
 
customers.For
 
example,
 
you
 
might
 
close
 
a

today
 
but
 
not
 
collect
 
payment
 
for
 
30,
 
60,
 
or
 
90
 
days,
 
while
 
still
 
covering
 
your
 
costs
 
in

A

flow
 
projection
 
accounts
 
for

the
 
12-Month
 
Cash
 
Flow
 
Statement
 
and
 
use
 
it
 
to

out

monthly
 
cash
 
position
 
through
 
your
 
first
 
year.

It's
